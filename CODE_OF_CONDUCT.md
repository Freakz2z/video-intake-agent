# Code of Conduct

We want a respectful, practical community for everyone. Be kind, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and respect the privacy of recordings and metadata.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, publishing someone else's private media or personal information, and persistent disruptive behavior are not acceptable. Report concerns to `freak050321@gmail.com`. Reports will be handled privately and may result in warnings, removal of content, or exclusion from project spaces.
